Hello there!
My name is Lea, I am 19 years old and I was born and raised in Düsseldorf. After graduating high school with the Abitur in 2014, I spent six months in London, where I was working as an Au Pair and exploring the city. Since October 2015 I am now living in Berlin and studying Communication Design at the HTW in Berlin-Oberschöneweide. This website features some of my works. Most of the things you can see here were made for my portfolio with which I applied at the HTW, but you can also find personal photos, little side-projects and random sketches. If you like what you see, I would be more than happy if you drop me a line at lea-becker@gmx.net.
I hope you enjoy!
