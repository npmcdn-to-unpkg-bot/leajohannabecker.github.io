CALENDAR










This was the first big project in the class 'Digital Media'. The task was to design an online calendar for an iPad. Our vision was to increase the user experience of a normal calendar by making a simple design with some small animations which are nice to look at.


UMDREHEN / DAS SELFIE-MEMORY








A project to the topic of "selfies". The idea was to work with the situation of the two cameras from your phone facing two directions you have when taking a selfie (the taking a picture of you and the one who sees what you see). I asked friends to take two of those pictures and then made a game out of them. It is called "UMDREHEN" (german for 'to turn around') which stands for the act of turning around the setting of the camera as well as the cards during the game.
